# Sources & References

## Ancient Sources

### Primary Greek & Roman Texts
- Homer. *Iliad*, circa 8th century BCE. (Brief references)
- Hesiod. *Catalogue of Women*, circa 7th-6th century BCE. (Fragmentary references)
- Bacchylides. *Dithyrambs*, 5th century BCE. (Theseus' journey)
- Euripides. *Cretans*, circa 430 BCE. (Lost play, fragments remain)
- Apollodorus. *Bibliotheca*, 1st-2nd century CE. (Epitome 1.7-9: Comprehensive account)
- Diodorus Siculus. *Library of History*, 1st century BCE. (Book 4: Theseus myth)
- Plutarch. *Life of Theseus*, circa 100 CE. (Detailed biography including Minotaur episode)
- Ovid. *Metamorphoses*, 8 CE. (Book 8, lines 152-182: Detailed narrative)
- Ovid. *Ars Amatoria*, 1 BCE. (References to Ariadne)
- Catullus. *Poem 64*, 1st century BCE. (Ariadne's lament)
- Hyginus. *Fabulae*, 2nd century CE. (Myths 38, 40, 41)
- Pausanias. *Description of Greece*, 2nd century CE. (Various references)

### Ancient Art
- Numerous Greek black-figure and red-figure vases (6th-4th century BCE)
- François Vase (570 BCE) — detailed Theseus scenes
- Roman mosaics depicting Theseus and the Minotaur
- Pompeian frescoes
